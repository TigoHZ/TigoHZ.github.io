FAQ's

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?
There are printers on every floor that you can use, just connect your laptop to the printer with this site and send the file you want to print through the printer.
How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?
The printers also have a scan option. Connect you laptop to the printer, place the document you want to scan on the scanner and scan with the scan app on your laptop.
How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?
If you go to the HZ web shop, you can select a bunch of items to add to your cart. You then fill in your information and pay for the items you selected. If you buy clothes and want to fit them first, you can contact Daphne Mesu at (d.mesu@hz.nl).
How can you book a project space in one of the wings?
Every student has acces to the self sevice portal. Here you can not only reserve Camera's or Beamers, but also project spaces. You can pick your own time frame, as long as it's open.
What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?
If you want to park at a HZ place you can go to the car park the Poelendaelesingel At the barrier you can ring the bell, after entering follow the signs marked with the HZ logo.
